Project: Boardwalk Games Website Design and Development

Boardwalk Games is a retro gaming store that specialises in classic video games and consoles. The project involved designing and developing a user-friendly website that showcases their products, provides information about the store, and allows customers to contact them easily.

Key Features:

  • Responsive Design: The website is fully responsive, ensuring a seamless experience across all devices, from desktops to mobile phones.
  • Product Showcase: A visually appealing product gallery that highlights the various games and consoles available at Boardwalk Games.
  • Contact Form: An easy-to-use contact form that allows customers to reach out for inquiries or support.
  • About Us Section: A dedicated section that tells the story of Boardwalk Games and its passion for retro gaming.

Technologies Used: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap for responsive design, and basic PHP for form handling.
